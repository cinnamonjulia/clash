## Handy planning takeaways (early read)

- **Almost everyone is off** for Winter Break (roughly **Dec 21 – Jan 1**) and **MLK Day
  (Jan 18, 2027)** — good region-wide event windows.
- **Spring breaks are staggered** across late March / early April, so there's no single
  region-wide spring window — worth a per-district check before scheduling.
- **SC districts start early** (mid-August) and **end early** (late May); NC/VA run later
  into June.
